# Tax-workflow summary: bad

You sold some stuff this year and made a bit of money, so you'll probably owe some capital gains
tax on it. Capital gains tax rates can vary quite a bit depending on your situation, so it's hard
to say exactly how much without doing the full math, but it's usually somewhere in the 15-20%
range for most people.

You also got some dividends, which are taxed too, though qualified dividends usually get a better
rate than ordinary income. Overall you should set aside a decent chunk of whatever you made just to
be safe, and remember that taxes are generally due sometime in the spring. If you're not sure about
any of this, a tax professional could help walk you through it in more detail closer to filing
season.
